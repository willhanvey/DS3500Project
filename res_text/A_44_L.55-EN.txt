2. Affirms once again that the continuation of colonialism in all its forms
and manifestations - including racism, apartheid, those activities of foreign
economic and other interests contrary to the Charter of the United Nations and the
Declaration on the Granting of Independence to Colonial Countries and Peoples, as

the complete and speedy eradication of colonialism and to the faithful and strict
observance by all States of the relevant provisions of the Charter, the Declaration
on the Granting of Independence to Colonial Countries and Peoples and the guiding
principles of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights;
