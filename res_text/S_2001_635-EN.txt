Draft resolution

The Security Council,

Having considered the question of the recommendation for the appointment of
the Secretary-General of the United Nations,

Recommends to the General Assembly that Mr. Kofi Annan be appointed
Secretary-General of the United Nations for a second term of office from
1 January 2002 to 31 December 2006.
