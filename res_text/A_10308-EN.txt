Report of the First Committee 

Rapporteur: Mr. Horacia ARTEAGA-ACOSTA (Venezuela) 

1. The item entitled "Implementation of the Declaration on the Strengthening 
of International Security: report of the Secretary-General" was included in the 
provisional agenda of the thirtieth session of the General Assembly in accordance 
with General Assembly resolution 3332 (XXIX) of 17 December 1974. 

2. At its 2353rd plenary meeting, on 19 September 1975, the General Assembly, 
on the recommendation of the General Committee, decided to include the item in 
the agenda and to allocate it to the First Committee for consideration and report. 

3. The First Committee considered the item at its 2054th to 2059th meetings, 
from 15 to 20 October 1975. 

4. The First Committee had before it the report of the Secretary-General 
(A/10205 and Add.l) submitted in accordance with paragraph 5 of resolution 
3332 (XXIX). The annex to the report contained a list of additional documents 
relating to this item which had been issued since the consideration of the subject 
by the General Assembly at its twenty-ninth session. In addition, the following 
documents were also circulated: 

(a) Letter dated 30 September 1975 from the Permanent Representatives of 
Romania and Tunisia addressed to the Secretary-General (A/C.l/1062); 

(b) Letter dated 16 October 1975 from the Permanent Representatives of 
Mexico and Romania addressed to the Secretary-General (A/C.l/1064); 

(c) Letter dated 17 October 1975 from the Permanent Representative of 
Romania and the Deputy Permanent Representative of the Syrian Arab Republic 
addressed to the Secretary-General (A/C.l/1065).
