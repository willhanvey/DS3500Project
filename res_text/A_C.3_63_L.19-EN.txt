  Moratorium on the use of the death penalty 
 
 

 The General Assembly, 

 Reaffirming its resolution 62/149 of 18 December 2007 on the moratorium on 
the use of the death penalty,  

 Welcoming the global trend towards the abolition of capital punishment, as 
recognized in the report of the Secretary-General,1 

 1. Welcomes the report of the Secretary-General on the implementation of 
resolution 62/149, and endorses the conclusions and recommendations contained 
therein;  

 2. Requests the Secretary-General to provide a report on the implementation 
of resolution 62/149 and the present resolution for consideration during its sixty-fifth
