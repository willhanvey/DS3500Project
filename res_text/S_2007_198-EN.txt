  Draft resolution 
 
 

 The Security Council, 

 Recalling its resolutions and the statements of its President concerning the 
Democratic Republic of the Congo, 

 Reaffirming its commitment to the sovereignty, territorial integrity and 
political independence of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, and its 
commitment to continue to contribute to the consolidation of peace and stability in 
the Democratic Republic of the Congo in the post-transition period, in particular 
through the United Nations Organization Mission in the Democratic Republic of the 
Congo (MONUC), 

 Noting that the situation in the Democratic Republic of the Congo continues to 
pose a threat to international peace and security in the region, 

 Acting under Chapter VII of the Charter of the United Nations, 

 1. Decides to extend the mandate and personnel strength of MONUC, as set 
out in its resolutions 1565 (2004), 1592 (2005), 1596 (2005), 1621 (2005), 1635 
(2005) and 1736 (2006), until 15 May 2007; 

 2. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
