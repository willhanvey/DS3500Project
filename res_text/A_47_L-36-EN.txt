Forty-seventh session
Agenda item 30

QUESTION OF PALESTINE

Afghanistan, Algeria, Comoros, Cuba, Guinea, Jordan,
Lebanon, Malta, Mauritania, Qatar, Senegal and Vanuatu:

draft resolution

The General Assembly,

Having considered the report of the Committee on the Exercise of the
Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People, 1/

Taking note, in particular, of the relevant information contained in
paragraphs 41 to 65 of that report,

Recalling its resolutions 32/40 B of 2 December 1977, 33/28 C of
7 December 1978, 34/65 D of 12 December 1979, 35/169 D of 15 December 1980,
36/120 B of 10 December 1981, 37/86 B of 10 December 1982, 38/58 B of
13 December 1983, 39/49 E of 11 December 1984, 40/96 B of 12 December 1985,
41/43 E of 2 December 1986, 42/66 B of 2 December 1987, 43/175 B of
15 December 1988, 44/41 B of 6 December 1989, 45/67 B of 6 December 1990 and
46/74 B of 11 December 1991,

1. Takes note with appreciation of the action taken by the
Secretary-General in compliance with its resolution 46/74 B;

2. Requests the Secretary-General to provide the Division for
Palestinian Rights of the Secretariat with the necessary resources, to
strengthen its programme of research, studies and publications through the
