  France: draft resolution 
 

 

 The Security Council, 

 Recalling its previous resolutions and statements on the Central African 

Republic, in particular resolutions 2121 (2013), 2127 (2013), 2134 (2014), 2149 

(2014), 2181 (2014) and 2196 (2015) as well as Presidential Statement 

S/PRST/2014/28 of 18 December 2014, 

 Taking note of the letter dated 29 January 2015 from the Secretary-General to 

the President of the Security Council,  

 Determining that the situation in the Central African Republic continues to 

constitute a threat to international peace and security,  

 Acting under Chapter VII of the Charter of the United Nations,  

 1. Decides to authorize an increase of 750 military personnel, 280 police 

personnel and 20 corrections officers for MINUSCA, in addition to the personnel 

authorized by paragraph 20 of resolution 2149 (2014);  

 2. Requests that the Secretary-General keep the new levels of military 

personnel, police personnel and corrections officers in MINUSCA under continuous 

review; 

 3. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
