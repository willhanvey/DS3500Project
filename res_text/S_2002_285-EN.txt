Letter dated 18 March 2002 from the President of the Security
Council addressed to the Secretary-General

I have the honour to inform you that your letter dated 13 March 2002
(S/2002/284) concerning your proposal to add Paraguay to the list of countries
contributing troops to the United Nations Peacekeeping Force in Cyprus
(UNFICYP) has been brought to the attention of the members of the Council. They
take note of the proposal contained therein.

(Signed) Ole Peter Kolby
President of the Security Council
