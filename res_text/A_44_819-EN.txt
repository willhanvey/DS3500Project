I. INTRODUCTION

1. The item entitled "Question of Antarctica" was included in the provisional
agenda of the forty-fourth session in accordance with General Assembly resolutions
43/83 A and B of 7 December 1988.

2. At its 3rd plenary meeting, on 22 September 1989, the General Assembly, on the
recommendation of the General Committee, decided to include the item in its agenda
and to allocate it to the First Committee.

3. The First Committee considered the item at its 42nd to 46th meetings,
from 20 to 22 November 1989 (A/C.1/44/PV.42-46).
