I. INTRODUCTION

1. The item entitled "Comprehensive system of international peace and security"
was included in the provisional agenda of the forty-third session of the General
Assembly in accordance with General Assembly resolution 42/93 of 7 December 1987.

2 . At its 3rd plenary meeting, on 23 September 1988, the General Assembly, on the
recommendation of the General Committee, decided to include the item in its agenda
and to allocate it to the First Committee.

3 . The First Committee considered item 73 jointly with items 71 and 72 at its
47th to 54th meetings on 23, 25 and 28 to 30 November 1988 (see A/C.1/43/PV.47-54).
