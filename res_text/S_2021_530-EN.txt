 The Security Council, 

 Having considered the question of the recommendation for the appointment of 

the Secretary-General of the United Nations, 

 Recommends to the General Assembly that Mr. António Guterres be appointed 

Secretary-General of the United Nations for a second term of office from 1 January 

2022 to 31 December 2026.
